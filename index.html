Hi, I'm Miko.

I am a Frontend Web Developer, originally from New York City, now based in Darmstadt. With a background in media studies and the arts, I bring a critical, yet empathetic approach to my work. 💻

As someone who was first online in the early 2000s, I always enjoyed building my own part of the internet even with just a little HTML. Never associating myself with being a "coder", I persued my other interests in the humanities and media, working for art and cultural institutions. While making a net art-inspired website from scratch for my master thesis project, I rediscovered my love for coding. I enjoyed not only the creative expression, but also the direct impact I could make through developing useful tools.

Through various online resources such as The Odin Project, Scrimba, and Udemy, I have learned how to write accessible, more-readable code and make fully responsive websites and apps. I am continuously learning essential problem-solving skills needed for programming the tools to help people work better. My childhood fascination with the internet has never left me and I am always curious about what new or alternative technologies are out there. 🔍
